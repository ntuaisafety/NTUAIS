Leonardo is an AI researcher at NTU's Speech Processing and Machine Learning Lab (GICE) under the supervision of Prof. Hung-yi Lee. With an MS in Electrical Engineering from NTU, his research focuses on NLP, Speech Processing, and Mechanistic Interpretability for multimodal LMs.

Between 2023 and 2024, he played a key role in developing localized Traditional Chinese LLMs. Transitioning into the AI safety domain, he has served as a core member of the NTU AI Safety Reading Group and a facilitator for the 2025 Taiwan AI Safety Summer Workshop.

A graduate of the BlueDot AIS technical course, he is deeply committed to advancing safety research within the Taiwanese AI ecosystem.

**Expected graduation date:** 2027
