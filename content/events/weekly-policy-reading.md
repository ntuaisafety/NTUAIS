Leveraging our connections with **NTU Law**, this track focuses on AI policy and governance with AGI preparedness in mind.

## What We Do

- Track global AI policy news
- Review trusted expert and blogger posts
- Lead clear, balanced discussions on AI regulation

## Session Format

Each 2.5-hour meetup includes:

| Phase | Activity |
|-------|----------|
| **Part 1** | Form 3-5 person discussion groups |
| **Part 2** | Answer pre-assigned questions from organizers |
| **Part 3** | Summarize key arguments and write short memos |
| **Part 4** | Cross-group debate and Q&A sharing |

## Who Should Join

This track is perfect for:
- Law students interested in tech regulation
- Policy researchers and advocates
- Anyone interested in AI governance
- Those preparing for AGI-related policy discussions
